Our Refer a Friend program is a simple way to receive discounts towards your orders with foodora and to send discounts to your friends.

Scroll down to see how it works!

1. You can invite your friends by sending then your unique referral link provided by this feature in the foodora app via Email , SMS WhatesApp or other messaging platforms

2. Your friends must be a new user and use the link you send to them to install the app and sign-up to receive their 50 kr credit, which will be automatically applied to their cart during cheakout.

3. When a friend places their first order using their referral credit, you will receive one credite of 50 kr that will be automatically applied to your art when you order . You voucher is valid for 3 months and your friends voucher is valid for 1 month .Both will be added to 'Your voucher'.

4. Only one credit for 50kr can be redeemed per order.

5. Credites are valid on orders of 150 kr or more.

6. Credits cannot be used in conjuntion with any offer or vouchers.

7. Credites may not be applied when ordering from resturents who do not accept vouchers

8. Vouchers can in some specific cases not be used when resturents are delivering the food themselves.

9. You will receive credites for up to 10 people you inite and who successfully makes their first order.

10.You can view all credites you've accumulated in your credit repository.

11. Foodora reserves the right to update these and conditions with effect for the future at any time.
